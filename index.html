About Us

We are a platform dedicated to connecting restaurants, NGOs, and individuals to reduce food waste by donating surplus food to those in need. Our platform serves as a bridge to prevent good food from going to waste and to help feed people who may be struggling.

Our mission is to create a sustainable solution where everyone can contribute to reducing food waste, feeding the hungry, and creating a positive impact on society and the environment.

How It Works

Our platform connects donors with people who need food. Here’s how it works:

  1. Step 1: Donors, such as restaurants or individuals, sign up on our platform and list the surplus food they wish to donate. You can offer any food item, whether cooked meals or fresh produce.
  2. Step 2: Recipients, such as NGOs or individuals in need, browse the available food donations on the platform. They can filter by location, food type, or delivery preference.
  3. Step 3: Once the recipient finds a suitable donation, they can request the food. The platform facilitates safe and efficient delivery between the donor and recipient.
  4. Step 4: Donors receive feedback from recipients and can track their contributions to see the impact they’ve made!
